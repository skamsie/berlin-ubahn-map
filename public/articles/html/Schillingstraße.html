On 21 December 1930, the former line E was opened with the Schillingstrasse station.

On the station's platform there is a work of art made up of several sculptures which was produced by young people from the Oranienburg Juvenile correctional facility. It was created under the supervision of the artist Ralf Schade and handed over to the (BVG) on 15 December 2003.
