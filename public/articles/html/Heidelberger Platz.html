Heidelberger Platz is a railway station in the Wilmersdorf district of Berlin. It is served by the U-Bahn line U3 and by S-Bahn Stations S41, S42 and S46.

The U-Bahn station opened on 12 October 1913 (arch.W.Leitgebel). With its high groin vault it is one of the most brightly decorated of the Berlin network. The eponymous square was named after the city of Heidelberg.
