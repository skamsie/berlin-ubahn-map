The Parchimer Allee underground station is a stop on the Berlin U7 underground line, which was opened on September 28, 1963. It is part of the first southeast extension of the U7 after the 1930s. The train station is located in the Berlin district of Britz across the eponymous Parchimer Allee and longitudinally below Fritz-Reuter-Allee.
