Frankfurter Allee is a Berlin U-Bahn station located on the U5.

History

The U-Bahn station was opened on 21 December 1930 and called Frankfurter Allee (Ringbahn) and was designed by the architect Alfred Grenander.

Originally the station was red, however it was changed to orange in the restoration of the station in the 1980s. After a second restoration in the 2004 the red colour was restored with new wall tiles.
