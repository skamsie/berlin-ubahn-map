Lindauer Allee is a Berlin U-Bahn station located on the U8 line. Designed by Rainer G. RÃ¼mmler, the station opened on 24 September 1994, as part of the extension of the U8 to Wittenau. The station is tiled in green, violet, light blue and yellow with trees depicted on the walls. Mentionable is the gallery with the balustrade from where the whole station is visible. The only similar galleried view in Berlin is at the station Rathaus Spandau.
