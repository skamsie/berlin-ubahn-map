Brandenburger Tor is an underground railway station in the central Mitte district of Berlin, Germany, located on the Unter den Linden boulevard near Hotel Adlon, Pariser Platz and Brandenburg Gate. It is served by the Berlin S-Bahn and U-Bahn line U5.

U-Bahn Station

This station was originally supposed to open for the 2006 World Cup. Problems with the groundwater delayed the construction several times, so that the opening could only take place on August 8, 2009.

The Oestreich / Hentschel office designed the station itself in anthracite and illuminated it with particularly bright ceiling lights. The platform with a double row of supports is 110 meters long and equipped with an elevator at the western end.
