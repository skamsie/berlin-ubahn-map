Voltastraße is a Berlin U-Bahn station located on the U8.

Built in 1914 but opened in 1930 (delayed by the First World War, it has granite columns and is 4.5 metres (15 ft) below the street.

After the completion of the berlin wall , the U8 was severely impaired. In the north, only the stations Voltastraße and Gesundbrunnen could be used by West Berliners. A tiny line of 2 stations that was little used by the Berliners - a condition that lasted until October 5, 1977 as the U8 was extended to Osloer Straße to interchange to the U9.
