Sophie-Charlotte-Platz is a Berlin U-Bahn station located on the U2.

History

The station was built by A. Grenander and opened in 1908. In 1935, the windows on the walls were removed. In 1938, the entrances of the station had to be moved. In 1988, twenty-six large paintings were attached to the walls showing the history of the subway before the First World War.

It was heavily damaged by air raids on 15 February 1944.
